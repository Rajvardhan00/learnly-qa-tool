LEARNLY SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
Document ID: SLA-002 | Version: 1.8 | Last Updated: March 2024

1. SERVICE AVAILABILITY
Learnly guarantees 99.9% uptime for all core platform services, measured on a monthly basis. This translates to no more than 43.8 minutes of unplanned downtime per month. The 99.9% SLA applies to the Learning Management portal, course delivery, grade book, and student authentication services.

2. SCHEDULED MAINTENANCE
Planned maintenance windows occur every Sunday between 02:00 AM and 04:00 AM UTC. Customers receive at least 7 days advance notice for scheduled maintenance. Emergency maintenance may be performed with 2 hours notice. Scheduled maintenance is excluded from uptime calculations.

3. INCIDENT RESPONSE TIMES
- Critical (P1) - Platform down for all users: Response within 15 minutes, resolution target 2 hours
- High (P2) - Major feature unavailable: Response within 1 hour, resolution target 8 hours
- Medium (P3) - Minor feature degraded: Response within 4 hours, resolution target 24 hours
- Low (P4) - Cosmetic/non-critical issues: Response within 1 business day, resolution target 5 business days

4. SUPPORT CHANNELS
Learnly provides 24/7 support via:
- Live chat (average response time: under 3 minutes)
- Email support: support@learnly.io (response within 4 hours)
- Phone support for Enterprise tier customers: 1-800-LEARNLY
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM) for institutions with 5,000+ active users

5. SLA CREDITS
If monthly uptime falls below 99.9%, customers receive service credits:
- 99.0% – 99.89%: 10% credit on monthly invoice
- 95.0% – 98.99%: 25% credit on monthly invoice
- Below 95.0%: 50% credit on monthly invoice
Credits are applied automatically to the next billing cycle.

6. DATA BACKUP & RECOVERY
All course data and student records are backed up every 6 hours. Backups are retained for 90 days. Recovery Point Objective (RPO): maximum 6 hours of data loss. Recovery Time Objective (RTO): platform restoration within 4 hours of disaster declaration. Backups are stored in geographically separate AWS regions (us-east-1 and eu-west-1).

7. PERFORMANCE STANDARDS
- Page load time: under 2 seconds for 95th percentile of requests
- Video streaming: adaptive bitrate with minimum 480p quality guarantee
- API response time: under 500ms for 99th percentile of API calls

8. EXCLUSIONS
The SLA does not apply to:
- Force majeure events (natural disasters, government actions)
- Customer-caused outages or misconfigurations
- Third-party service failures outside Learnly's control
- Beta or preview features
